NEW RHYTHM, STEALTH AND HORROR GENRE

Death By Misstep is an upcoming First-Person Rhythm, Stealth and Horror game set on a party cruise where a massive massacre by robots is happening.

Rhythm

Breathe to the beat of the music so the cruise ship's robots don't detect you!

Stealth

Find a way to leave the ship without dying... Don't let them see you!

Horror

Unveil the terrifying backstory of Jessica and the Samos cruise ship.

FAQ

When will Death By Misstep officially launch?
The estimated release date for Death By Misstep is Q4 2025.
Are there testing sessions? How do I access them?
Sure. We plan to conduct a heuristic playtest before the summer of 2025, which will not be accessible to the public. However, we will be participating in some events, such as Indie Dev Day, Guadalindie, and Bilbao, which we will announce on our social media.
What platforms will Death By Misstep be available on?
Death By Misstep will be available on Steam for Windows.
Will a demo be released before launch?
There is a possibility of releasing a demo at some point before the launch.
Who is developing Death By Misstep?
Death By Misstep is being developed by Misstep Studio, a young team with a project that originated in the first promotion of the pre-incubation program Level Up [Game Dev Hub] in Barcelona, Spain. We currently have some support from Amazon Web Services.

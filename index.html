FAQ

When will Death By Misstep officially launch?
The estimated release date for Death By Misstep is sometime in 2026.
Will it be possible to try the game before then?
Yes. A demo is already available on Steam, so look us up and give it a try! We will also be participating in events such as BCN Game Fest, where you can try out the latest version of the game. Stay tuned to social media!
What platforms will Death By Misstep be available on?
It will be available on Steam for Windows.
If I'm interested, is there anything you recommend me to do?
Of course, add us to your Wishlist on Steam! It helps us a lot, and you'll get a notification when we release the full game!
Who is developing Death By Misstep?
It is being developed by Misstep Studio, a young team with a project that originated in the first promotion of the Level Up [Game Dev Hub] pre-incubation program. Currently, we have members distributed throughout Spain. Want to get to know us? Check out the members section below!

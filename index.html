NEW RHYTHM, STEALTH AND HORROR GENRE

Death By Misstep is an upcoming First-Person Rhythm, Stealth and Horror game set on a party cruise where a massive massacre by robots is happening.

Rhythm

Breathe to the beat of the music so the cruise ship's robots don't detect you!

Stealth

Find a way to leave the ship without dying... Don't let them see you!

Horror

Unveil the terrifying backstory of Jessica and the Samos cruise ship.

FAQ

When will Death By Misstep officially launch?
The estimated release date for Death By Misstep is March 2024.
Are there testing sessions? How do I access them?
Yes, but they will be very few. We've planned to do a private heuristic playtest in early October 2023, which won't be accessible. However, we'll be present at Indie Dev Day 2023 and the Guerrilla Game Festival 2023.
What platforms will Death By Misstep be available on?
Death By Misstep will be available on Steam and Itch.io for PC (Windows). If we don't have much downtime, we're also thinking of getting it out on macOS.
Will a demo be released before launch?
We plan to release a demo for October/November 2023.
Who is developing Death By Misstep?
Death By Misstep is being developed by Misstep Studio, a young team with a project that originated in the first promotion of the pre-incubation program Level Up [Game Dev Hub] in Barcelona, Spain. Currently, we have some support with Amazon Web Services.
